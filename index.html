Since July 2020 staff have:

Attended 3,159 ferry services

Inspected more than 180,000 vehicles, boats and trucks

Staff have taken 2,179 consignments of honey. Unopened honey has been donated to Foodbank, with ~152 kilograms donated since December 2021.

Promotion of biosecurity has occurred through information in the KI Visitors Guide, various websites including SeaLink, KI Tourism Authority, KI Landscapes Board and PIRSA.

450,000 people

Social media has been used extensively with the “Keep me Sweet, Keep me Clean and Keep me Wild” campaign being run in every peak visitation period and had a reach of over 450,000 people.
